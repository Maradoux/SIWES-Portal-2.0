{% comment %} Internship Program {% endcomment %}

internship program

Our program is designed to provide SIWES IT students with hands-on experience and professional development

Program Duration

  • 3-6 month flexible program
  • Full-time and part-time options available
  • Start dates throughout the year
  • Possibility of job placement based on performance

Benefits

  • Hands-on experience with real projects
  • Mentorship from industry professional
  • Networking opportunities
  • Certificate of Completion
  • Potential job offer for top performers

Process

  • Applicaton and screening
  • Technical assessment
  • Interview with our team
  • Onboarding and orientation
  • Regular process evaluation
  • Final project presentation
{% comment %} Requirements {% endcomment %}

Requirements

What you need to join our SIWES internship program

Eligibility Criteria

  • Currently enrolled in an IT-related degree program
  • Knowledge of programming/tech concepts is not compulsory
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Good communication abilities
  • Ability to work in a team environment
  • Passion for technology and learning

Documents Needed

  • Updated resume/CV
  • Enrollment into one of the main IT programs in earlycode
  • SIWES application letter from educational institution
  • Portfolio of projects (if available)
  • School-issued ID
  • Proof of enrollment
